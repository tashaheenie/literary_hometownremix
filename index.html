Mythoterrestrial Destinations

Art: Pieter van Schalkwyk

Scadrial

In the world of Scadrial, you must visit the modern skyscrapers and canal-lined streets of the bustling metropolis of Elendel. For more daring travelers, include an excursion into the untamed Roughs featuring arid plains, rock outcrops, metal mines, and notorious saloons.

Art: Playground

Callipolis

Explore the Aurelian city of Callipolis. Nestled on the coast, this city is a beacon of power and tradition. It features grand castles, marble temples, and dragon stables. You may catch a glimpse of one of the Guardian dragon riders!

Art: Romain Kurdi

Roshar

Experience the thrill of highstorms from a safe distance in the tower of Urithiru, go climbing in the chasms of the Shattered Plains, and visit the city of Kholinar. Keep your eye out for legendary sprens that dance on the breeze, peek out of the fireplace, or swirl around your head in a moment of awe!

Prythian

Travel to Prythian, a land of breathtaking beauty and ancient magic, where each court offers a unique blend of enchantment and danger. In the hidden city of Velaris, you can stroll along riverfronts, explore vibrant markets, and bask in the peace and wonder of the City of Starlight.
